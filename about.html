About Us

Welcome to CK TechFix – Doorstep Tech GURU.

Who We Are

CK TechFix is a Lusaka-based mobile phone repair service that brings professional smartphone repairs directly to your doorstep. We specialize in Samsung, iPhone, and Google Pixel repairs, saving you the time and inconvenience of visiting a repair shop.

Founded by Chola Kachamba, CK TechFix was built on a simple idea: quality phone repairs should be convenient, affordable, and accessible to everyone.

Our services include LCD and OLED replacements, battery replacements, charging port repairs, back glass replacements, camera repairs, water damage diagnostics, software troubleshooting, and other hardware repairs.

At CK TechFix, we believe your smartphone is an essential part of your daily life. That's why we are committed to providing fast, reliable, and professional repair solutions with quality parts and exceptional customer service.

Our Mission

To deliver convenient, professional, and trusted mobile phone repair services directly to our customers.

Our Vision

To become Zambia's most trusted doorstep mobile phone repair brand.
